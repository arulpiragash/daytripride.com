About Us

Based in Paris, Groupe Blue Van Paris has been providing top-quality private transportation since 1998. Specializing in airport transfers, city travel and Disneyland park or hotels, we ensure a seamless, comfortable, and professional experience for every passenger. With a commitment to reliability and customer satisfaction, we serve key destinations, including Charles de Gaulle, Orly, Beauvais, central Paris, and Disneyland.

About Groupe Blue Van Paris

Groupe Blue Van Paris, since 1998, has become a reputable name in personal transportation within the dynamic landscape of France's capital. As a leading service provider, our focus extends beyond mere transportation across Paris and its scenic suburbs to the region's three major airports.

Our Core Competence

Our journey through the years has been marked by a steadfast commitment to excellence in service and customer satisfaction. We excel in transporting people across Île-de-France and have a deep understanding of the tourism sector. Upon special request, we also offer customized city tours, presenting an unparalleled view of Paris.

Collaborative Ventures

Our services are widely recognized and trusted by numerous travel agencies and corporate entities worldwide, who depend on our transportation solutions for their business needs.

Dedicated Team of Drivers

The backbone of our operation is our proficient network of independent drivers, each holding an official VTC card, which attests to their ability to meet the strict standards required for operating in Paris. Their extensive knowledge is a key asset.

Accessibility and Support

We prioritize staying accessible to our clients. Our office hours are dedicated to responding to emails and calls. Additionally, we offer a 24/7 emergency contact number to ensure that assistance is always just a call away, especially during your stay in Paris.

Our Fleet: Comfort and Eco-Friendly Options

Our commitment to a smoke-free environment extends to our well-maintained, air-conditioned 8-seater vehicles. For those seeking luxury, our premium 7-seater Mercedes vehicles are available subject to availability. Recognizing diverse needs, we also provide 4-seater vehicles, most of which are eco-friendly hybrids.
