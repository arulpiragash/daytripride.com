General Information

How do I choose the vehicle? ▼

You can choose from our fleet of vehicles based on your needs. We offer sedans for individuals, vans for families, and luxury vehicles for premium service. Select your preferred vehicle type during booking.

What type of vehicles do you use, and what are their features? ▼

Our fleet includes modern, well-maintained vehicles ranging from 4-seater sedans to 8-seater vans. All vehicles are air-conditioned, equipped with GPS, and maintained to high standards of cleanliness and comfort.

What destinations do you provide transfers to? ▼

We provide transfers to all major destinations in the Paris region including Charles de Gaulle Airport, Orly Airport, Beauvais Airport, Disneyland Paris, and throughout central Paris and suburbs.

How secure is my online payment? ▼

Your payment is fully secure. We use encrypted payment gateways with SSL certification. We accept major credit cards and payment methods with industry-standard security protocols to protect your information.

Why do I need to book in advance? ▼

Booking in advance helps us guarantee vehicle availability, assign the best driver for your journey, and provide you with accurate pricing. It also ensures we can meet you exactly when you need us.

I didn't receive a confirmation email for my reservation. What should I do? ▼

Check your spam or junk folder first. If you still don't see it, contact our support team at contact@groupebluevanparis.com or call +33 (0)1 43 32 70 81. We can resend your confirmation immediately.

Services for Children & Disabilities

Do you offer child seats and booster seats? ▼

Yes, we provide child seats and booster seats upon request. Please mention the age and weight of your child during booking so we can provide the appropriate safety seat.

Do you accommodate passengers with disabilities? ▼

Yes, we provide accessible vehicles and can accommodate passengers with disabilities. Please inform us of any special requirements during booking so we can arrange the best vehicle for your needs.

Booking & Cancellation

How do I book a ride? ▼

Booking is easy! Visit our website, select your pickup and drop-off locations, choose your preferred vehicle, and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with all details.

Can I cancel my booking? ▼

Yes, you can cancel your booking within the free cancellation window (typically 24 hours before your ride). Cancellations within this window are free. Late cancellations may incur charges.

Can I modify my booking? ▼

Yes, you can modify your booking details like time, location, or vehicle type if done within 24 hours of your scheduled ride. Contact our support team for modifications.

Pricing & Additional Services

What is included in the price? ▼

Our pricing includes professional driver, vehicle fuel, tolls, and basic insurance. It's a fixed-rate service with no hidden charges. Child seats and additional services are available upon request.

Do you offer discounts for group bookings? ▼

Yes! We offer special discounts for group bookings and multiple rides. Contact our team at contact@groupebluevanparis.com for a custom quote.

Do you offer hourly hire services? ▼

Yes, we provide hourly hire services for city tours, shopping, or custom itineraries. Minimum 2-hour booking applies. Contact us for hourly rates.

Airport & Train Station Pick-ups

Do you provide airport pickup services? ▼

Yes! We provide pickup services from all major Paris airports: Charles de Gaulle, Orly, and Beauvais. Our drivers monitor your flight status and will be ready when you arrive.

How early should I book my airport transfer? ▼

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for airport transfers. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings if vehicles are available.

Will the driver wait if my flight is delayed? ▼

Yes! We monitor your flight status and adjust pickup times accordingly. Delays are no problem – our driver will wait for you at the airport.

Do you offer train station pickups? ▼

Yes, we provide pickups from Paris train stations including Gare du Nord, Gare de l'Est, Gare de Lyon, and others. Book in advance for best service.

Where will the driver meet me at the airport? ▼

Your driver will wait at the meeting point in the arrivals hall with a sign displaying your name. Specific details will be provided in your confirmation email.

What are your office hours? ▼

Email support: 6AM - 11PM daily. Phone support: 24/7. We're always available to assist you with your booking needs.
